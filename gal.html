Overview

As a Theatre-Maker Nicole's practice is based in a holistic and spiritual human-centred community care focused way. She has extensive experience in directing/dramaturgy, facilitation and producing, working with professionals and communities. Nicole directed several short plays for Graeae Theatre’s Crips With Chips new writing showcase presented with Theatre by The Lake and Liverpool Everyman Theatre on their mainstages (2023-24). She is currently a Dramaturg Mentor on Paines Plough's Tour The Writer programme in partnership with Bradford Producing Hub and Bradford 2025. She was a Creative Associate on the Royal Shakespeare Company’s First Encounters production of Romeo and Juliet and Twelfth Night, where she assisted rehearsals in Stratford-Upon-Avon and directed the young ensembles in schools and associate theatres in the northern region of the tour (2024). Nicole has been trained by the Almeida Theatre, as a JMK Trust Mentored Director, through the Regional Theatre Young Directors Scheme (2021) and worked as a script-reader for Sheffield Theatres (2022).

Crips & Chips

Graeae Theatre & Liverpool Everyman Theatre

Nicole directed 2 new short plays as part of Graeae Theatres Crips With Chips New Writing showcase at Liverpool Everyman Theatres.

Gather Your Party Before Venturing Forth by Bobby Brill, performed by Nadia Nadarajah, Faye Donnelan and interpreted by Chandu Gopalakrishnan. The play explored long-distance friendships in the gaming world and insecure housing and disabled experience.

JANUS by Madeleine Farnhill, performed by Nadia Nadarajah, Mark Lockyer and Chandu Gopalakrishnan. A folk-horror exploring rural isolation, brutality and abuse through the appearance of a two-headed lamb.

Graeae Theatre

Graeae Theatre & Theatre by the Lake 2023

Nicole directed 2 new short plays as part of Graeae Theatres Crips With Chips New Writing showcase at Theatre by the Lake.

Let Me Fat in Peace by Caitlin Magnall-Kearns, performed by Beca Barton and Siobhan Athwal. This comedy explored fat-phobia and perceived desirability in mixed-weight relationships.

Ajar by Nell Hardy, performed by Beca Barton, Mariah Loca, Kellan Frankland and Charlie Folorunsho. The drama explored transphobia in women's refuges and unsafety in police handling.

Brief Encounters

Stephen Joseph Theatre (2022)

Nicole assisted the director on Emma Rice’s adaptation of the classic film, Brief Encounter in the round at the Stephen Joseph Theatre working with their Artistic Director. She provided contextual research, ideas and supported and observed the production across movement direction, musical direction and overall directorial vision.
